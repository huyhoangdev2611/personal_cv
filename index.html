Ronaldo Shows How A Single Player Can Inspire A Whole Team To Glory As Portugal Lift Euro 2016 Trophy

Zero wins in the group stage, lucky escape in the round of 16 and a thrilling win on penalties in the quarter-finals. If football is all about fighting till your last breath, then Portugal were worthy winners of Euro 2016 because they never gave up, even when things got real bad (that Ronaldo injury was a death-knell for them).

Read More

Portugal's triumph in Euro 2016 was historic not only because it was their first major international trophy, but also because of the way they achieved it. From Ronaldo's leadership both on and off the pitch to the strategic genius of coach Fernando Santos, every aspect of the team's journey was marked by resilience and determination.
